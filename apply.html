Apply to Volunteer

Thank you for your interest in volunteering with the Indie Film Collective. Volunteers help create a welcoming festival experience for filmmakers, guests, and community members.

Volunteer opportunities are open to people who enjoy supporting creative events, working with the public, and being part of a community-centered film experience.

Volunteer Positions

  • Guest check-in and ticket support
  • Concessions assistance
  • Theater ushering
  • Information table support
  • Event setup and cleanup

How to Apply

  1. Review the volunteer positions listed above.
  2. Choose the role that best matches your interests.
  3. Email the Indie Film Collective with your name, availability, and preferred volunteer position.

To apply, contact us at volunteer@indiefilmcollective.org with your availability and the position you are most interested in.
