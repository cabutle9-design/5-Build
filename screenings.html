Film Screenings

Showcasing independent films from emerging creators.

Audiences may discover the distinctive storytelling of Jane Schoenbrun, known for blending coming-of-age themes with psychological horror, and Raven Jackson, whose work explores identity, family, and memory through striking visual narratives. We also spotlight rising talents like A.V. Rockwell, whose character-driven stories have earned critical acclaim. By showcasing diverse voices and fresh perspectives, the Indie Film Collective aims to connect audiences with the next generation of filmmakers shaping the future of independent cinema.

Screening Highlights

  • Short films from local and student filmmakers
  • Documentaries focused on community stories
  • Post-screening discussions with creators

What Audiences Can Expect

  1. Fresh stories from new creative voices
  2. Opportunities to learn about the filmmaking process
  3. A welcoming space for film lovers and local artists
